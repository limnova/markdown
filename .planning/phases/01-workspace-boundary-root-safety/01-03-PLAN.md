<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

- `store -> controller`: persisted workspace keys may be stale, missing, or manually altered before startup restore.
- `controller -> Rust workspace loader`: accepted and restored paths cross into the native boundary checker.
- `active shell -> rejection feedback`: boundary failures must not replace trusted workspace content with foreign data.

## STRIDE Register

| threat_id | category | component | disposition | mitigation_plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-01 | Tampering | persisted `lastWorkspacePath` restore flow | mitigate | Re-run every stored path through `load_workspace` on startup and treat invalid results as blocked UI state, not trusted content. |
| T-03-02 | Denial of Service | invalid restore handling | mitigate | Keep a recoverable invalid-workspace panel with a visible reselect CTA instead of blanking the UI or entering a broken loop. |
| T-03-03 | Information Disclosure | boundary rejection handling in the active shell | mitigate | Show inline rejection feedback while preserving the current trusted workspace snapshot; never swap in out-of-root content. |
</threat_model>
